homogenizer

The Greerco homogenizer series offers a full range of tank-mounted, high shear mixers for laboratory to production scale. Used in scenarios where conventional agitation technology falls short of delivering the desired process outcome or production time.

The Homogenizer is suitable for small-scale R&D or full-scale batch operations. Homogenizers can be employed as alternatives or additions to traditional agitators in processes where de-agglomeration and dispersion operations are crucial.
