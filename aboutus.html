About Mullen

Since 1950, Mullen Equipment, based in Troy, Michigan, has been a trusted representative of industrial and chemical equipment across the Midwest. With decades of expertise, we have built a reputation for delivering exceptional service and results, generating significant sales for our principals across a diverse range of industries.

Our extensive product offerings and solutions cater to the Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Petrochemical, and Industrial sectors, as well as Municipal solutions, Food & Beverage, and Paper & Pulp industries. From specialized process equipment to comprehensive system solutions, we are dedicated to meeting the unique needs of our customers.

At Mullen Equipment, we are committed to expanding our capabilities and providing outstanding service to both our customers and principals.

At Mullen Equipment, we believe in delivering quality, reliability, and solutions that drive our clients' success. It’s our commitment to excellence that has defined us since 1950.

In loving memory of
