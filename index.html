ABOUT MULLEN EQUIPMENT CORPORATION

Founded in 1950, Mullen Equipment started as a small industrial equipment distributor. Over the years, the company faced numerous challenges, from economic recessions to shifts in industrial technology. Yet, through it all, they not only survived but thrived by maintaining a clear focus on two key principles: innovation and customer service.

Mullen Equipment Advantage:

Our Industry focused services

One defining moment in their history came during the rise of environmental regulations in the 1990s. Many industrial companies struggled to adapt to new sustainability standards, but Mullen saw this as an opportunity.

Over 75 Years of Industry Leadership

Since 1950, Mullen Equipment has been a trusted representative and distributor for leading process solution manufacturers across the chemical, pharmaceutical, petrochemical, and industrial sectors

Comprehensive Product Range

Mullen offers a wide range of advanced equipment, including mixers, static mixers, heat exchangers, vacuum pumps, pumping systems, and water treatment solutions. This allows them to serve diverse industries with highly specialized needs

Pioneers in Sustainable Solutions

Mullen is at the forefront of providing environmentally-friendly and energy-efficient systems, including dry scrubbers, FRP ventilation systems, and wastewater treatment equipment, all aimed at reducing environmental impact

Cutting-Edge Infrared Thermal Technology

They provide state-of-the-art infrared thermal systems for early fire detection, thermoforming, injection molding, and plastic joining, showcasing their commitment to innovation

Customized Engineering Solutions

Mullen’s in-house engineering team offers tailored system designs, troubleshooting, and equipment analysis to optimize processes and reduce operational costs, ensuring that every client receives solutions fit for their unique needs

Serving Major Industries

Including chemical processing, pharmaceutical manufacturing, food and beverage, paper and pulp and municipal systems, Mullen has built a reputation for reliability and innovation in critical industrial processes
